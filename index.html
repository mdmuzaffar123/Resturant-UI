Our Story

A Taste of Heritage

Established in 2001 by Chef Muzaffar, The Golden Spoon has been a pillar of fine dining in the city. Our philosophy is simple: source the freshest, local ingredients and treat them with the respect and artistry they deserve.

From our signature slow-cooked lamb to our seasonal vegetarian specials, every dish is crafted to deliver a unique and memorable flavor profile. We invite you to be part of our culinary journey.
